Upcoming Project

TechBoost Ghana Project

TechBoost Ghana, an initiative by Impact Horizon, focuses on empowering SMEs in the Ga South District of Ghana through digital transformation. This project aims to provide comprehensive business support, leveraging technology to enhance SMEs' capabilities in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Duration: 2-3 months per session | Location: Ga South District, Ghana

Key Activities include Digital Business Process Transformation, Online Marketing Strategies, Financial Technology Solutions, and more.
